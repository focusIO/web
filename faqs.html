Frequently Asked Questions

FOCUS+ is an application that strives for improvement in the community through integrating innovations in life sustainment by bridging skills gaps through a single click.

In order to access the available training in FOCUS+, you must first register for an account. For detailed steps on how to register and enroll for a training, please click this link to create your account.

FOCUS+ is available for FREE. There are trainings that are freely offered, however, there are also trainings that are only accessible with payments, depending on the organization that will host the endeavor.

Certification will be given upon the fulfillment of the training. For trainings that have fees, certificates are provided upon fully paid dues.
