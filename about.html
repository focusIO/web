ABOUT

FOCUS+ is an online platform that offers training to help Filipino workers improve their technical skills and education. Using modern technology, FOCUS+ makes it easy and convenient for learners to access technical and vocational training whenever and wherever it is desired.

WHAT'S ON FOCUS+?

FOCUS+ offers efficient and affordable training programs that provide easy access to improve learner's skills and advance one's capabilities. These program aims to bridge skills gap within the society through a click.
